About us

Through Turn., health care providers will find a simple yet effective solution to over-crowded waiting areas, uneasy staff workers, all while permitting a personalized approach to tending patients, before they enter the actual doctorâ€™s office.

Our studies have show that 83% of people find waiting at medical offices troublesome, as well as a influential factor in the decision of scheduling an appointment. 91% of the same population stated that they would be more inclined to frequent their medical service providers, if a solution to the waiting inconvenience existed.

Turn. allows its users to monitor their assigned turn at a health service provider, all on real time. Patients will be able to use their accustomed waiting time however they please. Thus eliminating the adverse limitations that come with seeking medical attention.
